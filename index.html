About COHERENCE'19

Coherence a platform celebrating every vision that creates innovations.To be Staged first in the year 2019, COHERENCE is expected to stand as the most spectacular techno-management fest of the region. Rising to a higher stratum of science every year, the fest has chronicled its glory all over the country. Events, workshops and exhibitions hosting a convergence of intelligence and technology have witnessed the footfall of thousands of young minds. COHERENCE 2019 is all set to raise the curtain, and this time a new sci-fi Odyssey will beat the drum.

WHAT FACTORS DRIVE COHERENCE 2019?

Science Fiction

COHERENCE 2019, one of the largest techno-management fests of India, is on an odyssey to stretch out one's imaginations to the far reaches, and extract the technology that one sees in their mind's eye.

Out-of-box

Team COHERENCE aims at breaking down boundaries and thinking out of the box. After all, science fiction is all about letting your imagination run wild and capturing them into spectacular inventions.

Social Responsibility

With the world moving at a breakneck pace, it is important to ensure that everyone moves together. Team COHERENCE recognizes the need to uphold our social values and virtues to achieve true development.
